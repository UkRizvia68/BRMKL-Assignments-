Popular Dishes of Pakistan

Bhindi Gosht

Bhindi Gosht is a flavorful combination of okra and meat cooked toghter.It is made witH tender pieces of meat(such Mutton or beef)cooked with bhindi and a blend of spices.It's hearty dish that pairs welL with rice,naan OR roti.

Biryani pulao

Biryani pulao is a delicious rice dish cooked with aromatic spices&meat.It is actually a combination of biyani and pulao.

Kheer

Kheer is a sweet rice pudding made with milk,rice & sugar.Adding almonds,coconut and raisins to kheer enhances its flavour even more.

Nihari

Nihari is a classic Pakistani dish that's popular for its rich & flavorful gravy.It's typically made with slow_cooked meat , aromatic spices and served with naan or rice.

Sohan Halwa

Sohan halwa is a delicious and popular sweet treat in Pakistan.It is made with a combination of sugar ,milk,ghee and a variety Of nuts Like almonds ana pistachioS.

Payee

Payee is a popular Pakistani dish made from the feets of cows,goats,or sheep,It is cooked in a flavorfil or mildly spiced broth and is typicakky served with naan or toast.It's a delicious and popular dish often enjoyed during special occasions Eid_ul_Adha and in weddings for breakfast.

Saag

Saag is a traditionaL Pakistani dish made from leafy greens,such as spinach or mustard greens ,cooked with combination of spices.It's a flavorful & nutritious dish that is often servrved with cornbread and Makkhan.

Gajrella

Gajrella is a popular Pakitani dessert made from carrots .It is typically prepared during the winter season and is sweet in taste .It is cooked with milk,sugar,carrots,khoya and dates.

Sajji

Sajji is a popular dish from Balochistan ,Pakistan.It's made by marinating a whole chicken with spices and then slow_roasting it until it's tender and juicy.

Chapli Kabab

Chapli kabab is a mouthwatering Pakistani dish.It originated from from the KPK region and is made from ground meat,usually beef or mutton,mixed with various spices or herbs.the mixture is shaped into flat,round patties and then fried untill crispy.The unique flavour comes from ingredients like crushed pomegrantate.seeds, coriander,cuminand chopped onions.
