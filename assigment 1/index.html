Threek Labiak Pakistan

Tehreek-e-Labbaik Pakistan ( تحریکِ لبیک پاکستان) is a far-right Islamic extremist political party in Pakistan. The party was founded by Khadim Hussain Rizvi in August 2015. It was the fifth largest party in Pakistan at the time of the 2018 Pakistani general election, and secured over 2.2 million votes.
